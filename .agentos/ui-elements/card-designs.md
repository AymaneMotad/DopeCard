## Implementation Guidelines

1. **Consistency**: Maintain consistent design language across card types
2. **Branding**: Allow business customization while maintaining readability
3. **Accessibility**: Ensure color contrast meets WCAG standards
4. **Mobile Optimization**: Design for small screen sizes (wallet display)
5. **Platform Differences**: Adapt for Apple Wallet vs Google Pay

## Color Guidelines

- Use brand colors from business
- Ensure sufficient contrast (4.5:1 minimum)
- Test in both light and dark modes
- Consider colorblind accessibility

## Typography

- Clear, readable fonts
- Appropriate sizing for mobile
- Hierarchy for information
- Support for multiple languages
